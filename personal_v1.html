About Me

Welcome! I am an undergraduate student studying at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, with a major in Computer Science and Statistics. My interest lies in the intersection of mobile computing, human-computer interaction, and machine learning. I am especially interested in human-centered signal processing, including wireless localization and audio/image processing. During my undergraduate study, I have been fortunate to work with Professor Kevin Chau, Professor Alanson Sample, and Professor Emily Mower Provost. I also had a great experience collaborating with some wonderful graduate students. Outside academia, I train for triathlon, and I enjoy playing the piano.
