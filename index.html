About Me

Welcome! I am a first year Ph.D. student at Duke University, advised by Professor Maria Gorlatova. My research interest lies in Mobile Computing and Mixed Reality, especially in their intersection with Human-Robot Collaboration. Previously, I obtained Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, with minor in Statistics. During my undergraduate study, I have been fortunate to work with Professor Kevin Chau and Professor Alanson Sample.
